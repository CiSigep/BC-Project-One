will take you to a whole new world...

Bryce Canyon National Park

Bryce Canyon National Park, a reserve in southern Utah, that is known for crimson-colored hoodoos, which are spire-shaped rock formations.

Aurora Borealis

From september to april, Iceland is a hotspot for this magnificent lightshow. The Aurora Borealis, is more commonly referred to as the Northern Lights.

Camel Riding in India

One of the most unforgettable and enchanting experiences you can have in India is to ride through the desert on camel back and camp out under the stars.

Florence, Italy

Florence, capital of Italy’s Tuscany region, is home to many masterpieces of Renaissance art and architecture. One of its most iconic sights is the Duomo.

London, England

Home to the royals, birthplace of high tea and posh accents. At its center stands the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations.
